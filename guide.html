Voice Guide

Every singer has tells. This guide distills the community's ear for each idol — what their voice sounds like, the giveaways that out them in a mix, and the moments where it shines. Tap a Hear it chip to jump straight to that moment in the player.

Every voice profile here is adapted and condensed from throwaway93257's Love Live music primers, posted to r/LoveLive & r/SchoolIdolFestival (2015–2018). The ear for every tell is theirs; any errors in our adaptation are our own.
